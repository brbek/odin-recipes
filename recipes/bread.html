Only wholemeal my brother

Very bread, yes

  • Wholemeal flour (anykind)
  • Wholemeal flour (any other kind)
  • Wholemeal flour (any third kind)
  • dry yeast, one small packet
  • salt
  1. Throw it all in a bowl
  2. Mix it
  3. Add some yeast with mild water
  4. continue adding water until you have one mud mass in your hands
  5. Make two mud bricks and bake them at 200°C or 4365°F for 40min
